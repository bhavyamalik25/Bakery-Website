Our Story

A little bakery at
the edge of the woods.

Maplewood Bakery was born on a cold October morning in a kitchen that smelled of cinnamon and old books. We bake everything by hand — no shortcuts, no preservatives, just premium organic ingredients and recipes passed down through seasons.

Come in, stay a while. Let the smell of fresh bread and the crackle of the oven remind you that some things are still made with love.
